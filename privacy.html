Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how our Discord bot (“Project Vanguard”) collects, uses, and stores information when you interact with it.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Messages: The Bot may process your messages to execute commands. However, it does not store message content permanently.
  • Discord User IDs: Your user ID may be temporarily used for functionality such as admin checks or message attribution.
  • Voice Channel Data: The Bot accesses voice state information to enable music features, but it does not record or store voice data.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to commands and provide interactive features like playing music or sending responses.
  • To identify authorized users (e.g., for moderation or admin-only commands).

3. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data permanently. Any necessary data is stored in memory temporarily and discarded after use.

4. Third-Party Services

This bot may interact with third-party APIs such as YouTube for music functionality. These services may collect data as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

5. Your Consent

By using the Bot, you consent to the terms in this Privacy Policy.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the bot owner via Discord.
